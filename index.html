Bangladesh is a country located in South Asia. It is bordered by India to the west, north and east, and by Myanmar to the southeast. To the south, it has a coastline along the Bay of Bengal. The country is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including the Bengal tiger, the national animal. Bangladesh is known for its rich cultural heritage, including its ancient history, literature, music, art and architecture. The country has a population of over 160 million people, making it one of the most populous countries in the world. The official language is Bengali, and the capital city is Dhaka. Bangladesh is a developing country, with a rapidly growing economy. It is a member of the United Nations, the Commonwealth of Nations, and the South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation.
